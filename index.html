Chakra visualisation

  • Crown Chakra

    Top of the head.

  • Brow Chakra

    Centre of the forehead, the 'Third eye'.

  • Throat Chakra

    Point of the neck between shoulders and head.

  • Heart Chakra

    Centre of the chest, back of the ribcage.

  • Solar Chakra

    The chakra core, centred above and behind the navel.

  • Sacral Chakra

    Centred on the sacrum between the hips.

  • Root Chakra

    At the root of the spine, tailbone.
